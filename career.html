Sales Engineer — Power Electronics
Sales & Business Development
Full Time Bengaluru 2–5 Years

We are looking for a motivated Sales Engineer with a background in power electronics or electrical engineering to drive sales of our programmable power supply products across South India and develop new business across industrial and R&D sectors.

Key Responsibilities
  • Identify and develop new business opportunities in industrial, EV, aerospace, and R&D sectors
  • Conduct product demonstrations and technical presentations to customers and OEMs
  • Prepare and follow up on technical proposals, BOQs, and quotations
  • Maintain strong relationships with key accounts and support repeat business growth
  • Collaborate with application engineers to understand and address customer requirements
  • Attend trade exhibitions and technical seminars as required
Requirements
  • B.E./B.Tech in Electrical, Electronics, Power Electronics, or Instrumentation Engineering
  • 2–5 years of experience in technical sales, field applications, or power electronics
  • Knowledge of programmable power supplies, test & measurement equipment preferred
  • Strong communication, presentation, and negotiation skills
  • Willingness to travel across South India as required
  • Proficiency in CRM tools and MS Office
Application Engineer — Power Systems
Engineering & Applications
Full Time Bengaluru 1–4 Years

We seek an Application Engineer to provide pre-sales and post-sales technical support for our complete range of programmable AC/DC power supplies, electronic loads, and battery simulators.

Key Responsibilities
  • Provide application engineering support to customers during evaluation, integration, and commissioning
  • Conduct product demonstrations, POC setups, and technical training sessions
  • Develop application notes, test procedures, and technical documentation
  • Coordinate with ET System Germany for technical escalations and product updates
  • Support the sales team with technical specifications and tender responses
  • Perform on-site commissioning and troubleshooting at customer facilities
Requirements
  • B.E./B.Tech in Electrical, Electronics, or Power Electronics Engineering
  • 1–4 years in power electronics applications, test systems, or R&D
  • Hands-on experience with power supplies, oscilloscopes, and test benches
  • Familiarity with USB, LAN, CAN, RS232, GPIB communication interfaces
  • Good analytical and problem-solving skills; ability to work independently
Service & Repair Technician
Service & Support
Full Time Bengaluru 2–6 Years

We are hiring a Service Technician to handle in-house and on-site repair, calibration, and preventive maintenance of our programmable power supply products across customer sites in India.

Key Responsibilities
  • Perform diagnosis, repair, and calibration of programmable DC/AC sources and electronic loads
  • Execute preventive and corrective maintenance at customer sites
  • Document service reports, fault analysis, and corrective action records
  • Manage spare parts inventory and coordinate procurement with our Germany partner
  • Support customers with warranty and AMC (Annual Maintenance Contract) servicing
Requirements
  • Diploma or B.E./B.Tech in Electronics, Electrical, or Instrumentation Engineering
  • 2–6 years of hands-on experience servicing power electronics or laboratory equipment
  • Knowledge of analog and digital circuit troubleshooting, PCB-level repair
  • Experience with soldering, component-level repair, and electrical safety procedures
  • Valid two-wheeler driving licence preferred for customer site visits
