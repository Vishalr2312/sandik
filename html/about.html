Our Story

Built on German
Engineering

Sandik Technologies Pvt. Ltd. is a specialized engineering company focused on delivering high-performance programmable AC/DC power supply solutions and advanced testing systems to industrial and R&D sectors. Established on October 29, 2025, the company builds on a strong foundation of technical expertise and industry experience, evolving from earlier operations under ET System India.

We actively support research and development initiatives in collaboration with ET System Electronic GmbH, Germany, delivering reliable solutions tailored to complex testing and power requirements. Our offerings include sales, application support, and servicing of programmable power supplies, ensuring high efficiency, accuracy, and compliance with global standards.

At Sandik Technologies, we believe that advanced technology combined with strong technical support is key to delivering effective solutions. Our team is committed to providing complete support — from product selection and configuration to after-sales service — ensuring long-term reliability and customer satisfaction.

Mission, Vision & Values

Our Mission

To deliver complete power testing solutions — from product selection and configuration to commissioning and after-sales service — ensuring long-term reliability and customer satisfaction through advanced German technology with strong local engineering support.

Our Vision

To be India's most trusted partner for programmable power supply solutions, enabling innovation across EV, renewable energy, aerospace, defence, and R&D sectors by combining ET System's world-class products with Sandik's local expertise.

Our Values

Engineering excellence, customer-first approach, technical integrity, and continuous improvement drive everything we do. We believe the combination of advanced technology and strong application support creates lasting partnerships built on trust.

Technical Partnership

Powered by German Engineering

ET System Electronic GmbH has been manufacturing high-precision laboratory power electronics since 1986. With their ISO 9001:2015 certification and an in-house manufacturing depth that spans metal processing to EMC compliance, they deliver the highest quality power electronics to customers worldwide.

As India's authorized technical representative, Sandik Technologies brings this same German precision and reliability to Indian customers — with full local application support, commissioning, and service.
