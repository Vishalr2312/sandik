Service &
Support

Comprehensive technical support, calibration, repair, and maintenance services to keep your power systems at peak performance.

Our Services

Complete Support Portfolio

Application Support

Pre-sales and post-sales application engineering support to configure, integrate, and optimize your power supply system for your specific test application. Available remotely or on-site.

Installation & Commissioning

On-site installation, setup, and commissioning of new equipment to ensure proper operation from day one, including interface configuration, software setup, and initial verification testing.

Repair & Diagnosis

Expert fault diagnosis and repair of programmable power supplies and electronic loads. We maintain access to OEM spare parts through our ET System Germany partnership for fast turnaround.

Calibration Services

Traceable calibration of voltage, current, and frequency outputs to ensure measurement accuracy and compliance with your quality management requirements. Certificates provided.

Preventive Maintenance

Scheduled preventive maintenance visits to inspect, clean, and verify performance of your equipment — minimizing unplanned downtime and extending service life of critical test assets.

Product Training

Hands-on training for your engineers on product operation, software interfaces, and safety procedures. Available at our Bengaluru office or on-site at your facility. Remote sessions also available.

03
Service Quotation

For physical repair or calibration, we provide a clear quotation covering parts, labour, and estimated turnaround time before any work begins.

04
Repair or On-Site Visit

Equipment is repaired at our Bengaluru service centre or our engineer visits your facility, based on the nature of the service required.

05
Verification & Handover

All serviced equipment is tested against specifications before handover. A full service report is provided documenting all work performed.
