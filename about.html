Our Story

Built on
Engineering Services

Sandik Technologies Pvt. Ltd. is a specialized engineering company focused on delivering high-performance programmable AC/DC power supply solutions and advanced testing systems to industrial and R&D sectors. Established on October 29, 2025, the company builds on a strong foundation of technical expertise and industry experience, evolving from earlier operations.

We actively support research and development initiatives in delivering reliable solutions tailored to complex testing and power requirements. Our offerings include sales, application support, and servicing of programmable power supplies, ensuring high efficiency, accuracy, and compliance with global standards.

At Sandik Technologies, we believe that advanced technology combined with strong technical support is key to delivering effective solutions. Our team is committed to providing complete support — from product selection and configuration to after-sales service — ensuring long-term reliability and customer satisfaction.
